ğŸ’¡ Key Insights

Home & Kitchen has the highest number of listed products, indicating strong market saturation.

The Mouse category has limited product diversity, presenting potential opportunity for new sellers.

Office Supplies has the highest average rating (~4.8), reflecting strong customer satisfaction.

Home & Kitchen products are heavily discounted, which could be optimized to improve profit margins.

Mobile Accessories receive the maximum discounts, indicating intense price competition.

Cables maintain strong ratings and could sustain lower discounting.

TVs & Accessories show the lowest ratings, highlighting the need for product innovation and quality improvement.
